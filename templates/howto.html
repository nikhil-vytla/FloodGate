SMS Ping System

How to report via SMS

Step 1:

Spot rising water levels.

Step 2:

Text FloodGate at +1 (201) 482-3830 and use the keyword 'report' followed by the 'type', 'severity' and 'location' properties each separated by a space.

Step 3:

Our server accepts the following format for crowd-sourced data: "report [water/trash/electricity] [LOW/MID/HIGH] ±#.##,±#.##".

(Ex. report water HIGH 7.38,-2.21)

How to check for nearby trash

Step 1:

Text FloodGate at +1 (201) 482-3830 with format "query [coordinates] [search_radius]".

Step 2:

Receive a text containing the closest recent report within your search radius in the format "[distance] [coordinates] [type] [severity]".

Step 3:

Help your local community out!
